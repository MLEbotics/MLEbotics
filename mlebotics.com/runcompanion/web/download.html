About MLE

Built by a runner, for runners.

Runner Companion is the flagship product of MLE (Making Life Easier) — a brand-new, Calgary-based Canadian startup at the intersection of AI and automation. Founded by Eddie Chongtham, a passionate long-distance runner who wanted something that didn't exist: a robotic pacer, a tireless training companion, and a smart mobile aid station — all in one.

Eddie's vision is simple — use cutting-edge AI to remove the friction from everyday athletic and personal challenges. Runner Companion is just the beginning. MLE is early-stage and growing fast, with a mission to bring intelligent automation to real-world human experiences.

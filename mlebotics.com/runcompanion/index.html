// AI RUNNING COMPANION

RunCompanion

An AI-powered running coach in your pocket. GPS tracking, real-time Bluetooth heart rate, Gemini AI coaching, voice guidance, and a physical robot running companion — all in one app.

// WHAT IT DOES

Built for serious runners

🤖
AI Running Coach
Gemini AI analyses your run in real-time and delivers voice coaching, pace alerts, and post-run summaries.
📍
GPS Tracking
Live route recording with map view, distance, pace, and elevation — all stored to Firebase for history.
❤️
Bluetooth Heart Rate
Connects to your Garmin watch or any BLE heart rate device. HR zones and real-time feedback during the run.
🔊
Voice Navigation
Text-to-speech announcements for pace, distance, HR, and AI coaching tips — no need to look at your phone.
🦾
Robot Companion
Controls a physical ESP32-based robot that runs alongside you — streams live camera video and mirrors your pace.
📊
Run History
All workouts synced to Firestore. Review route maps, pace splits, HR data, and AI coaching notes after every run.
